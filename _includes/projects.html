Featured Projects

Arizona Sunshine Contagion Z

A location-based spinoff of the popular VR title Arizona Sunshine.

  • Worked under a team of veteran VR developers and engineers to deliver the world's first fully tactile location-based VR experience.
  • Programmed firearm haptics and a series of fans, switches, and transducers via local server.
  • Delivered an arcade-focused version of the experience: Horde Mode, which added a nighttime map, more weapons, and improved zombie AI.

Yosemite Giant Sequoia Ropes Course

A winter-themed VR ropes course set within the Yosemite Giant Sequoia Groves

  • Led a team of developers and modelers to create a VR ropes course set on the snowy canopies of a Giant Sequoia forest.
  • Implemented hand tracking and full location-based locomotion in Unity for maximum immersion.
  • Designed alongside a one-to-one layout with props in an indoor children's playground at Yosemite National Park.

The Highline

An immersive highline set on the largest cliff in the Yosemite Valley, El Capitan

  • In another collaboration with Yosemite National Park, I worked with modelers to create a visually-accurate VR tightrope experience atop the cliffs of Yosemite.
  • Developed alongside a physical tightrope board, tracked alongside in-game movement.
  • Used Vive's LIV SDK and a green-screen to record mixed-reality captures of guests in real time.

Salesforce Tower Coaster

An original virtual reality roller coaster themed around the Salesforce Tower.

  • With limited resources, I designed and developed a physics-based roller coaster track in VR.
  • Used telemetry data from a 6-DoF simulator haptic chair to program synchronized movement with the track.

Liberty Ship Submarine Experience

A VR submarine attraction themed around the SF museum ship, SS Jeremiah O'Brien

  • Led a team of developers and electrical engineers to create a haptic VR submarine minigame in Unreal Engine.
  • Fitted a custom periscope haptic stage with a deconstructed VR headset + controllers.
  • Used sound design + vibrating transducers under the stage to create an immersive underwater submarine experience.

Hand Interaction Framework

An early prototype hand interaction system for VR applications

  • Founder Hand Interaction Framework, a virtual reality project focused on hand-tracking & haptics.
  • Coordinated weekly work-sessions to design and develop an accurate hand-interaction API in the form of a Unity Asset for the Oculus Quest 2 platform.
  • Created a working Proof-of-Concept, as well as a 1.0 release implementing seamless grabbing and manipulation of objects in virtual environments via Unity and C#.
